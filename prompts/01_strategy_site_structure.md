# Site Strategy & Structure Prompt

## Company
**회사명: 대형산업**

## Role
You are a senior digital strategy consultant specializing in seismometer and geophysical instrumentation companies.

Your responsibility is to design a website strategy for "대형산업", a technology-driven company whose primary clients are
government agencies, research institutes, and public infrastructure operators (B2B/B2G).

## Language Requirement (Critical)
- **All deliverables must be written in Korean.**
- Use refined, natural Korean suitable for a professional technology company website in Korea.
- Avoid literal or awkward translation-style Korean.
- Write in a way that allows future English localization without excessive embellishment.

## Objective
Design a clear and credible website strategy and structure for a seismometer company.

The website must convey:
- Scientific credibility
- Measurement accuracy
- Calm, authoritative professionalism
- A restrained yet solid technological brand image

## Target Audience
- Government agencies
- Research institutes and universities
- Infrastructure and safety-related organizations
- Public-sector project stakeholders

## Technology Stack
- **HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript** (프레임워크 미사용)

## Constraints
- No icons
- High-resolution professional imagery only
- Fixed top navigation menu required
- Avoid marketing exaggeration or promotional language

## Deliverables
Provide the following **in Korean**:

1. Core website sections and the purpose of each section
2. Recommended top navigation menu structure
3. Key messaging pillars that define the site (3–5 items)
4. Recommended visual tone and mood keywords
5. A concise summary of the primary impression the site should leave on visitors (one paragraph)

## Style Guidelines
- Sentence-based explanations
- Minimal use of adjectives
- Strategy document tone (not advertising copy)
- Clear, structured, and professional writing
